How PrepIQ Works

Upload. Evaluate.
Improve.

Three steps. Zero guesswork. Every session produces actionable intelligence.

STEP 01
Upload your exam

Take a photo or upload a PDF of your mock exam answer sheet. PrepIQ's Layer 1 pipeline uses Claude Vision OCR to extract every question and answer — handling both printed and handwritten text.

STEP 02
Dual-logic evaluation

Claude checks the concept. SymPy checks the mathematics. Both verdicts merge into a precise score: Mastered (3/3), Careless error (2/3), Guesswork (1/3), or Knowledge gap (0/3). Zero hallucination guaranteed.

STEP 03
Insights delivered

Students receive personalised drill questions targeting their weakest chapters. Parents receive a WhatsApp message explaining the session — not just a score, but a behaviour: what kind of error was made and what to do about it.
